A Tribute To...

10 Inventions That Changed The World:

  1. Alternating Current - Winner of the 1893 G.E. - Westinhouse feud, this system developed by Tesla, generates power and distribution throughout North America.
  2. Light - While he didn't invent light itself, Tesla did invent how it could be harnessed and distributed. Tesla developed flourecent bulbs in his lab 40 years before the rest of the industry was using them.
  3. X-Rays - Ever had an x-ray before? Well thank Tesla for that one too! While researched heavily in the 1800's, Tesla was essential to researching the entire spectrum leading up to the x-ray.
  4. Radio - In 1943, the Supreme Court overturned a patent for Guglielmo Marconi after it was proven Tesla invented the radio years prior.
  5. Remote Control - A relative of radio, in 1898 the first remote controlled model boat was demonstrated.
  6. Electric Motor - Seen one of those cars driving around town brandishing his name? It can also be found in industrial fans, household applicances, water pumps, machine tools, and power tools just to name a few.
  7. Robotics - Tesla is quoted saying “I have by every thought and act of mine, demonstrated, and does so daily, to my absolute satisfaction that I am an automaton endowed with power of movement, which merely responds to external stimuli.” Yes, Tesla was first to develop a concept of robotics.
  8. Laser - It is undeniable that the invention of the laser has helped mankind in positive ways through infinate ways. Imagine surgery, construction, or our advancement of military machines and military defense systems are being developed as directed energy weapons.
  9. Wireless Communications - J.P. Morgan funded Tesla to build the world's first tower that used natural frequencies to transmit data, which included information in the form of images, voice messages, and text.
  10. Limitless Free Energy - Aside from the cost of Tesla's wireless tower, he had proven that the universe is filled with energy that can be harnessed and utilized to create a world wide web to connect people everywhere. The best part about this frequency, it's free!
